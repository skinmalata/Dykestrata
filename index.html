About Us

Engineering Confidence
From the Ground Up

DykeStrata is a leading geoservices firm specializing in subsurface investigation, geological mapping, and geotechnical consulting. We bridge the gap between raw geological data and actionable engineering intelligence.

Founded with a commitment to scientific rigor and client-centric service, our team of licensed geologists, geophysicists, and geotechnical engineers deliver comprehensive solutions for infrastructure, mining, environmental, and energy projects across the globe.

Our integrated approach combines field expertise with cutting-edge technology to provide accurate, cost-effective subsurface characterization that de-risks your projects and drives informed decision-making.
